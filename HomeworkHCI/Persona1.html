This person was chosen because she is in daily contact with images, this being the target of the application, she has a great experience in this field and can be a potential user of the application. In addition, the application can make her work easier both personally and professionally. For example:

  • Experience with certain products(images);
  • Working environment;
  • Social connections;
  • The user's goals.
Goals

The user's goals are:

  • Practical Goals: To identify certain objects in the pictures that are not very clear in order to make the work easier, to do it faster and more efficiently.
  • Personal Goals: To save time to do as much as she can, to avoid frustration due to misinterpretations
Frustrations and Pain Points
  • UI simplistic;
  • The theoretical information on the site is useless, it is not necessary to use the application;
  • Sometimes she is not sure which of the 3 identification options is better, so she always uses one and never the other two, even if the information on the site explains.
Detailed Description

This person is secondary because the application finds immediate uses in the field in which they work. Being a student at that faculty, she often works with images, so she has a fairly high level of experience with them and will see the usefulness of the application immediately. In its case, the greatest utility is when it wants to restore certain defective images to be as high quality as possible, so the application indicating the object in the image can save a lot of time and make things easier. In addition to saving time by automating the process of recognizing image content, it will also avoid stressful situations. This field makes her have experience with other tools so she did not encounter any weight during the use of the application. Its only problem with the application being on the UI side does not prevent it from continuing to use it, although the technical part of the information is somewhat extra for it. In conclusion, her experience has been positive and she can be a frequent user of the application (website).
